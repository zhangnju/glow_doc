whether the provided F conforms to the backend-dependent graph constraints. Giving the backend an opportunity to check that everything conforms to its specific restrictions by overriding this function. It is highly recommended for backends to make their backend specific verifications a super-set of target independent Function::verify() by calling it in their overridden implementation.
